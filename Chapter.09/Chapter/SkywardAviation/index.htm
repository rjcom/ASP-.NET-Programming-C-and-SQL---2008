Skyward Airlines is one of the world's largest airlines with over 1,200 daily departures. We serve more than 1,000 cities in all 52 states and more than 150 countries on six continents. We operate a fleet of more than 500 aircraft including Boeing 747s and 757s, McDonnell-Douglas DC-9s, and Airbus A330s, A320s, and A319s. Skyward is also one of the world's largest cargo airlines, operating a dedicated fleet of 18 B747 freighters.
