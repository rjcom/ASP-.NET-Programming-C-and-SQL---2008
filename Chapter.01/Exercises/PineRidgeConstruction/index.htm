Pine Ridge Construction

Pine Ridge Construction provides comprehensive general contracting, construction management, project development, and consulting services on types of building projects. Founded in 1922, Pine Ridge Construction Company has built a solid reputation for market excellence, drawing on a rich history and a strong heritage of quality, stability, and innovative leadership. Today, we are one of the largest construction firms in Central Valley, employing over 300 people, and the largest ISO 9001 certified construction firm on the West Coast.
